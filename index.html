Who We Are

Where the hills breathe
and healing begins

Breath of Bandarawela is a wellness and spa resort located in the beautiful hill country of Bandarawela. Our resort provides a peaceful environment where guests can relax, heal, and reconnect with nature.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's leading wellness and eco-tourism destination.

Our Mission

To provide holistic wellness experiences while promoting sustainable tourism.
